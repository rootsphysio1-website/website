Specialist Therapists

Sports Injury : Role of Physiotherapy in Rehabilitation

Sports physiotherapists play a vital role in athletic rehabilitation by supporting injury recovery, enhancing performance, and educating athletes on preventing future injuries. Our comprehensive sports physiotherapy services include:

Advanced Pain Relief Therapies

Advanced pain-relief modalities such as TENS, IFT, ultrasound therapy, laser therapy, cold packs, and heat therapy to reduce discomfort and inflammation.

Joint & Soft Tissue Mobilization

Specialized hands-on techniques to relieve pain, improve joint mobility, and support muscles, including the application of Kinesio taping.

Rehabilitation Treadmill Training

Access to a specialized treadmill facility designed to assist in rehabilitation, gait correction, and performance improvement.

Pilates & Core Conditioning

Targeted exercise programs to enhance core strength, stability, flexibility, and overall body control.

Sports Screening & Performance Conditioning

Detailed assessments followed by customized conditioning programs to boost performance and reduce the risk of injuries.

Sports Massage Therapy

Therapeutic massage techniques aimed at promoting recovery, relieving muscle tightness, and improving circulation.

Sports Rehabilitation & Injury Prevention Programs

Structured rehabilitation programs that ensure a safe and effective return to sport while minimizing the chances of re-injury.

Why Choose Roots Physio & Rehab Clinic?

Leading the Way in Specialized Rehabilitation

Roots Physio & Rehab Clinic is Kolkata's premier physiotherapy center dedicated exclusively to neurological and spinal rehabilitation. Our specialized focus allows us to provide unparalleled expertise in these complex conditions. At our clinic, we follow evidence-based physiotherapy techniques that are scientifically proven and clinically effective. Our treatments are based on the latest research in physical therapy, combined with years of hands-on experience. This approach ensures faster recovery, reduced risk of recurrence, and safe rehabilitation for all age groups.

Founded by neuro-physiotherapy specialists with international training, we combine advanced Western rehabilitation techniques with personalized care suited to Indian patients. Our multidisciplinary team includes neurological physiotherapists, spinal specialists, occupational therapists, and rehabilitation physicians. From pain management to advanced rehabilitation protocols, every therapy plan is carefully designed, monitored, and modified according to your progress.

Our Commitment: Every patient receives an individualized treatment plan based on comprehensive assessment, latest research evidence, and their personal recovery goals.
